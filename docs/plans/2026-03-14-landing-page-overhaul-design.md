# Landing Page Copy Overhaul — Design

**Goal:** Rewrite the contractor app landing page to reflect all 4 services (Inspect, Decode, Supplement, Quote), updated pricing ($10 decode / $50 supplement), and workflow-centric positioning.

**Target audience:** Roofing company owners/managers evaluating a platform for insurance restoration work.

**Tone:** Professional authority with natural industry insider language. No jargon dumps, no bro marketing. Think Stripe's design sensibility meets a roofing contractor's vocabulary.

**Visual design:** Dark theme (#060A13) stays. Cyan (#00BFFF) and green (#39FF9E) accents stay. Layout restructured around job workflow.

- 3 free, then $50 each

**Step 4 — Quote**

- Free, unlimited
- 55+ shingle product lines across 6 manufacturers

### 4. Why 4Margin (3 differentiators)

**"The logic adjusters can't deny"**
We identify every missing line item with the correct Xactimate code, backed by IRC references and manufacturer specs. You get the justification and documentation to build your supplement.

**"Built on code authority"**
Every supplement item is backed by IRC building codes, manufacturer installation requirements, and industry standards. Not opinion — documentation adjusters can't dismiss.

**"Carrier pattern intelligence"**
We track which items each carrier approves and denies, by region. Your supplement is built to match what actually gets paid — not just what should be covered.

## Critical Copy Rules
- **NO exact pricing claims.** Supplements show recovery ranges, not Xactimate pricing. Quotes show market-based pricing ranges (±5%).
- **Supplements provide logic + justification + documentation.** Contractor builds the final estimate in Xactimate.
- All language is educational, never legal/insurance advice.
