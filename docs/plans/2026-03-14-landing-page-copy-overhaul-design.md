# Landing Page Copy Overhaul — Design Doc

**Date:** 2026-03-14
**Approach:** Service-Led (Approach A)

## Problem

The current landing page is over-indexed on insurance restoration contractors (~40% of target market), over-markets AI as the value prop, and has no revenue path for retail roofers who use inspections and quotes.

## Key Decisions

- **Audience:** All roofing contractors — retail and insurance restoration equally
- **Tone:** Direct and confident. No fluff, no buzzwords, no "AI-powered"
- **Pricing:** Freemium base → two subscription tiers → custom volume
- **Coverage:** MD, PA, DE (43 counties). Keep the map.

## Page Structure

### Section 1: Hero

- **Headline:** "One platform for every roof."
- **Subhead:** "Inspect. Quote. Decode. Supplement. 4Margin keeps your jobs organized from first look to final payment."
- **Stats row:** 6 Manufacturers · 43 Counties (MD · PA · DE) · 4 Services · Free to start
- **CTA:** "Get Started Free"

### Section 2: Services (4 cards)

**Inspect**
"Walk the roof, tap submit. Get a professional inspection report with photos, measurements, and manufacturer specs — ready to hand the homeowner or send to the carrier."
Free — unlimited.

**Quote**
"Build accurate quotes with real product lines from 6 manufacturers. Material breakdowns, labor, waste — all in one document the homeowner can say yes to."
Free tier, then subscription.

**Decode**
"Upload any insurance policy. Get a plain-English breakdown of what's covered, what's excluded, and what the carrier owes — before you start the job."
Pay-per-use or subscription.

**Supplement**
"Missing line items, underpaid trades, code-required work the adjuster skipped. 4Margin finds what's owed and builds the supplement with code authority and manufacturer backing."
Pay-per-use or subscription.

### Section 3: Why 4Margin (4 value prop blocks)

1. **Everything in one place** — "Your inspections, quotes, policies, and supplements — all tied to the same job. No more jumping between apps or digging through email."
2. **Built for the field** — "Designed for contractors between jobs, not office workers at a desk. Fast, mobile-ready, no learning curve."
3. **Code and manufacturer authority** — "Every supplement and inspection report is backed by IRC building codes and manufacturer installation requirements — not opinions."
4. **Your jobs, your data** — "Every document, photo, and report lives in your account. Organized by job, accessible anytime."

## What's Removed

- All "AI-powered" / "10-Layer Engine" / "Claude" language
- "Built for insurance restoration contractors" framing
- Per-use standalone pricing ($15 decode, $149 supplement)
- "Most restoration contractors juggle disconnected tools..." copy
- Emoji-heavy feature cards
- Enterprise tier (replaced by "Contact Sales" for volume)

## What's New

- Services-first structure (4 cards for 4 services)
- Retail roofer monetization via Pro tier ($99/mo for unlimited quotes + decodes)
- Knowledge base as a Pro feature gate
- Free supplements (3) in free tier
- Direct, confident tone throughout
- Volume/custom pricing option
